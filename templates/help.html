Help & User Guide

How To Use ToDoFlow

1. Register an Account

Go to the Register page and fill out all the required details such as username, email, and password. Once registered, you can log in and access the system.

2. Login

Enter your email & password on the login page. After logging in, you are redirected to the Home page.

3. Creating a Task

On the Home or My Tasks page, click on “Add Task”. Enter the task name and optional notes. Press Save to store it.

4. Editing a Task

Click on a task and choose Edit. You can update the name, notes, and change the status between Pending and Completed.

5. Deleting a Task

Open a task and click the Delete button. This permanently removes it from your list.

6. Profile Page

Here you can view your registered information and manage your account.

7. Switching Theme

Open the menu (⋮) and click Toggle Theme. The theme is automatically saved and applied next time.

8. Logout

Use the Logout option in the menu to safely exit your account.
