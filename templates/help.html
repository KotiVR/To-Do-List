How To Use ToDoList

1. Register an Account

Create your account with username, email, and password.

2. Login

Use your email & password to log in and access your dashboard.

3. Creating a Task

Press Add Task, enter the task name and optional notes, then save.

4. Editing a Task

Open any task → press Edit → update → save changes.

5. Deleting a Task

Open a task → press Delete to remove it permanently.

6. Profile Page

View your details and manage your settings.

7. Switching Theme

Open the menu (⋮) → choose Toggle Theme.

8. Logout

Open menu → click Logout to securely sign out.
